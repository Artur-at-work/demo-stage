About

Judy was born in Taipei in 1971 and have been travelling between Taiwan and the United States for many years. She is a full-time mother and homeschooling her five lovely children.

Books

She is an author of best selling parenting book - "My 100 Years Old Pedeatritian", where she introduced Dr. Denmark’s methods to Mandarin speaking readers. Her parenting advice has changed lives for thousands of Taiwanese families. In following years several more books were published to provide even more detailed guidance for parents.

Baby Food

I have had a dream since a long time ago: to turn Dr. Danma’s baby food puree into a convenient bag. I remember that I had to spend more than 3 hours in the kitchen every week to prepare a week’s worth of baby food, and it was very inconvenient to travel with the food. With this motivation, it was planned a few years ago, and after 18 months, the dream finally came true. This product is called "babyfood" (babyfood), which has another blessing meaning Baby.

Music

Judy studied classical piano from an early age and has competed with many top pianists in several American and International piano contests. She then diverted her interests into gospel music and started to integrate pop elements into her music compositions. She graduated from Department of Music of Indiana University and later earned a masters degree in teaching English as a second language from Columbia University's Teacher's College in 1995.
